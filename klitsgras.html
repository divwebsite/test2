Klitsgras is known for its vibrant atmosphere and unique events, which range from live music performances to outdoor cinema screenings, food markets, and more. The venue features several stages and open-air areas, allowing for multiple events to take place simultaneously.

One of the standout features of Klitsgras is its focus on sustainability and eco-friendliness. The venue is powered by solar panels and has a strict waste management policy to reduce its environmental impact.

In addition to its regular events, Klitsgras also offers camping facilities for visitors who want to stay overnight. The campsite is equipped with facilities such as showers and toilets, and visitors can choose between standard and luxury camping options.

Overall, Klitsgras offers a unique and memorable experience for visitors looking for an outdoor event venue with a focus on sustainability and eco-friendliness.
